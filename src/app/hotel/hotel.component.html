This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Mermaid Hotel & Club is located at Mahawaskaduwa fishing village in Kalutara, about 39 km from Colombo. This tropical retreat features 2 outdoor pools, a health spa and free parking. Rooms at Mermaid Hotel are simply furnished and come with private balconies or terraces. Each has a CD player and satellite TV. Hot tea, coffee and snacks are available with the minibar. Sahana Spa features relaxing massage treatments, as well as yoga and aerobics classes. Guests can play a game of water polo or join water aerobics class at the swimming pool. Mermaid also provides a games room. Overlooking the ocean, Mermaid Hotel’s restaurant serves fresh seafood dishes. Drinks and cocktails can be enjoyed at any one of the 4 hotel bars. Mermaid Hotel & Club is 74 km from Colombo International Airport. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.4 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
